Hotel Granados 83

A bit about Hotel Granados 83

Hotel Granados 83 is a Boutique Hotel in Barcelona's city center. The hotel houses fine art ranging from the building's own industrial revolution Catalan style, reproducing the area's textile factories. To Hinduist and Budhist pieces. The restaurant itself is decorated with original Roman mosaics in the traditional geometric style.
